# MediVision

**English**

MediVision is a smart medication integration tool designed to bridge the gap during care transitions (e.g., moving from hospital to home). Patients often struggle to reconcile new discharge prescriptions with their existing home medications. MediVision solves this by using AI to scan documents and pill bottles, automatically generating a unified, safe, and easy-to-read daily medication schedule.

**Key Features:**

- **AI-Powered OCR:** Uses Google Gemini Vision to read text from discharge summaries and round pill bottles (ignoring complex ingredient lists to focus on dosage instructions).
- **Unified Schedule:** Automatically merges instructions into a clear timeline (Morning, Noon, Evening, Bedtime).
- **Drag & Drop Upload:** Simply drag images of your documents or medicine bottles onto the screen.
- **PDF Export:** Download a clean, printable PDF of your medication schedule with one click.
- **Modern Design:** Features a clean, Apple-style aesthetic with interactive "Nexus Hexagon" cursor effects.
- **AI Branding:** Dynamically generates a unique cartoon doctor logo on startup.

**中文 (Chinese)**

**MediVision (医药视界)** 是一款智能药物整合管理工具，专为解决患者出院后的“护理过渡”(Care Transition) 痛点而设计。很多患者在出院时手握新的处方单，却不知道如何将其与家中现有的旧药结合服用。MediVision 利用人工智能技术，为您自动整理并生成一份统一的每日服药时间表。

**主要功能：**

- **智能 OCR 识别：** 调用 Google Gemini 视觉模型，精准识别出院单据和药瓶上的文字（自动过滤复杂的成分表，专注于提取服用剂量和频率）。
- **自动生成时间表：** 将不同来源的药物自动归类，生成清晰的早、中、晚、睡前服用计划。
- **拖拽上传：** 支持直接将药瓶照片或单据图片拖入网页进行识别。
- **PDF 导出：** 一键将生成的服药时间表保存为 PDF 格式，方便打印或分享。
- **极简设计：** 采用苹果风格（Apple-style）的极简 UI 设计，配合“Nexus Hexagon”六边形光标特效，提供流畅的用户体验。
- **AI 动态图标：** 每次启动时自动生成可爱的 3D 卡通医生图标。
